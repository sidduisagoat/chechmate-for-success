CHECKMATE FOR SUCCESS

Using Chess to Develop Focus, Patience, Emotional Regulation and Strategic Thinking

In Partnership with Shaping Therapies

Explore Our Journey

About The Project

Checkmate For Success was a community project conducted in collaboration with Shaping Therapies. Through structured chess sessions, we worked with children to strengthen focus, patience, confidence, planning, decision making and emotional regulation. By teaching a small group of students consistently over several weeks, we were able to adapt our lessons to individual needs and create meaningful long term impact.

Community Need

Focus

Many children experience difficulty maintaining attention for long periods.

Patience

Impulsive decisions often affect learning and emotional regulation.

Decision Making

Children benefit from opportunities to think through consequences.

Confidence

Structured success experiences help build self belief and resilience.
